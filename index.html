Facts About Starfish

  • Lone starfish, like most starfish, are echinoderms and have five (5) arms radiating from a central disc
  • They can regenerate lost limbs, and in some cases, an entire starfish can regrow from a single limb if part of the central disc remains
  • Starfish use a water vascular system for movement and feeding, operating tube feet with hydraulic pressure
  • They are predators and mainly feed on bivales like clams and muscles by everting their stomachs to digest prey externally
